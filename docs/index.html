DS MUSIC.

Support

DS Music is a client for your own Synology Audio Station. It streams music from a Synology NAS that you control — it is not a streaming service and has no catalog of its own.

Getting started

  1. Make sure Audio Station is installed and running on your Synology NAS.
  2. Open DS Music and enter your NAS address (e.g. a QuickConnect or DDNS URL, or a local IP), your Synology username and password.
  3. Browse Artists, Albums, Playlists or Search, and play.

Troubleshooting

  • Can’t connect: verify the NAS address is reachable from your device’s network and Audio Station is enabled for your user.
  • No artwork: cover art is read from the songs’ embedded tags on the NAS.
  • Playback stops in background: ensure the app has not been force-quit; lock-screen controls are supported.
