Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 May 2026

DS Music (“the app”) is a client for your own Synology Audio Station. We designed it to keep your data on your devices and your own server.

Data we collect

None. The developer does not collect, store, transmit to us, or share any personal data. There is no analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDK in the app.

Credentials & server address

The Synology server address and the username and password you enter are stored only on your device (in the iOS Keychain / the app’s local storage). They are sent only to the Synology server you specify, in order to authenticate and stream your music. They are never sent to the developer or any third party.

Music & network

Audio, metadata and artwork are fetched directly from the Synology NAS you connect to. The app communicates only with that server.

Children

The app is not directed to children and collects no data from anyone.

Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated date.
